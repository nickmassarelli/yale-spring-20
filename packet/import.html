bookmark toolbox™

This website is a place for publishing and sharing browser bookmarks.

Bookmarks are typically used in a practical way, but I wonder what happens when they are used unconventionally. Can bookmarks become a structure for writing, curating, documenting, or citing?

All are invited to submit collections of their own. Contributors are encouraged to consider naming conventions, folder structuring, and sequencing.

Each contributor will receive physical bookmarks in the mail printed with their collection title.
